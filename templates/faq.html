Frequently Asked Questions

Our Answers

Q: Do I have to pay for shipping?

  A: Shipping is free, the only extra expense paid is tax. Everything else is on us.


Q: If I am not satisifed with the product, can I return it?

  A: If the product has any issues or any dissatifaction, you may return it and get 100% of your money back guarantee.


Q: How do I check on the status of my order?

  A: You may view the status of your order by logging into your PacifiCoast Apparel account and clicking on past orders. Check your order status with your order number and email address.


Q: Can I order by phone?

  A: Unfortunately, we do not take orders over the phone at this time. However, you may order directly from our online website or on your smartphone.
