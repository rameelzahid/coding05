About Us

Our Story


Our company was founded in 2018 by two entrepreneurs with a vision of bringing west coast style to everyone around the world. We were inspired by many people who have the desire of living the California lifestyle. With the dreams of others and our passion for fashion, our team created PacifiCoast Apparel. Our mission statement, "Live and Feel like the Coast.", is a message that can hopefully bring our west coast upbringing and fashion influences to the world and inspire many to live and feel the way we do, by the coast.
